Hey, I'm Nefise — I like making things.

Sometimes that means designing interfaces, sometimes it's organizing creative events, and sometimes it's filming random moments that turn into stories. I'm a kinesiology student who somehow ended up falling in love with design, storytelling, and the messy process of figuring things out by doing.

I get restless if I'm not learning or creating something new — whether that's a short film, or a pop-up event that gets people sharing their weirdest skills. I like fast-paced environments, big ideas, and projects that feel a little chaotic at first but come together in unexpected ways.
